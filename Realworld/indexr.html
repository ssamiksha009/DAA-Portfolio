Real World Application in DAA

Convex Hull Algorithm in Tumor Size Detection

Demo

This project introduces an innovative utilization of the Convex Hull algorithm to precisely delineate tumor boundaries using provided tumor coordinates. The Convex Hull algorithm constructs the smallest convex shape encompassing these points, facilitating accurate mapping of tumor spatial distribution within the body. By leveraging the computational efficiency and robustness of the Convex Hull algorithm, the project significantly enhances medical diagnosis and treatment planning. This approach offers healthcare professionals valuable insights into tumor localization and distribution, aiding in more informed decision-making. Moreover, the adoption of Convex Hull in this project underscores its potential for advancing medical imaging and diagnostic practices. By providing a precise method for tumor boundary delineation, the project lays the foundation for more effective interventions and personalized treatment strategies. This innovative application of Convex Hull not only enhances patient care but also paves the way for further advancements in medical data analysis and healthcare outcomes.
